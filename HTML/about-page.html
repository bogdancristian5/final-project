Who am I ?


I was born and raised in Alba Iulia. My favorite hobby is traveling. I like all kinds of travel: by car, by train, by plane, and traveling on foot. This passion started in 2017, when I took part of Erasmus Student Exchange, in Czestochowa, Poland, where I spent three months of my life at Czestochowa University of Technology, as a trainee. This was a life changing experience for sure. I got to know sooo many people from the whole world, and it was the best way to get out of my comfort zone.

In the summer of 2018, I was part of the US Work And Travel Summer Program in Hyannis, Massachusetts, where I tried different jobs such as Cashier/Fast Food Crew Member at Dunkin Donuts, or Dishwasher/Line Cook at British Beer Company Hyannis, where I worked for more than three months. In 2019 I graduated “1 Decembrie 1918” University of Alba Iulia, "Finances and Banking" profile at the Faculty of Economic Sciences. But it wasn't enough for me, it wasn't what I was looking for.

That's why, in August 2019 I came to Cluj Napoca to start a new journey, so I decided to try my luck at "Scoala Informala de IT" where I was accepted in the same month. After 5 months here I am, ready to graduate and start looking for a job based on the same profile.
